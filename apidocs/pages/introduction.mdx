A multi-tenant, RBAC-gated platform for running AI evaluations, building messaging
and voice **workflows** over your CRM and clinical data, and asking questions of
your results in plain language.

This documentation has two parts:

- **Documentation** (this tab) — concepts, architecture, and step-by-step guides:
  running evaluations, connecting providers and CRMs, building workflows, uploading
  datasets, configuring nodes, and using the assistant.
- **API Reference** — the live OpenAPI reference for every endpoint, with an
  interactive playground.

> progressively from the live system.
